The works of Ta-Nehisi Coates

While Ta-Nehisi Coates is well known for books and articles on social justic issues, such as "Between the World and Me"1, few people are aware that he was actually the writer for the most recent reboot of the "Black Panther" comic book series2. While comics are a major departure from his normal literary contributions3, Black Panther as a comic series focuses on issues of race and power in ways that are somewhat atypical for other comics4, making Coates' involvement more characteristic.
